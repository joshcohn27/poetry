So...Uh, Yeah

            A crutch to dodge the awkward pause,
            The Irish goodbye of stories' cause.
            Three little words, they slip away,
            But do we lose the magic they convey?
            
            An ending should be clear, yet kind,
            A thread that ties what's left behind.
            What's a film without its close?
            What's a series if the finale goes?
            
            So I beg of you, please take care,
            And learn to end with style, with flair.
            For without a close, we drift, we stray—
            So… uh, yeah. FUCK, what more to say?
