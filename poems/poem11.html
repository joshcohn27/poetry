Personal Perception

I'm the bright light for others
Turning bad moods into good ones
Creating smiles out of thin air
Just by being me

But I look in the mirror
And don't feel that same warmth
In fact I feel the opposite
I drain my own spirit

If I'm so loved by others
Why is it so hard to love myself
I hate what I see
I dislike my own perception 

But I'm clearly great, right?
The people that I love and respect
The ones I'm around most 
All love me so much

It really makes no sense
Why it's so hard
Oh wait
I have anxiety and depression

Fuck mental health issues
